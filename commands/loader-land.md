# loader.land — CLAUDE.md Template Marketplace

You now have access to loader.land, a web-based marketplace for sharing CLAUDE.md templates and AI assistant settings.

## Available Actions

Tell me what you'd like to do:

1. **Browse templates** — Search the community gallery for CLAUDE.md templates, skills, and markdown files
2. **Upload settings** — Backup your AI assistant configuration for transfer to another machine (24h code)
3. **Download settings** — Restore settings from a transfer code
4. **Store & share markdown** — Publish your CLAUDE.md template or skill for the community
